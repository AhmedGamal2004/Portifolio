My Workflow

How the Magic Happens

1

Concept & Ingestion

Step 01

We sync up on the video goal. You upload your raw files (A-roll, B-roll, audio). I download, log, and organize the files in my system to prepare for an efficient workflow.

2

The Assembly Cut

Step 02

I trim down the footage to the absolute best takes, pacing the story logically to build the core spine of the video. No dead air, no fluff.

3

Visual & Sound Polish

Step 03

This is where we add keyframes, motion titles, color correction, and sound design. This phase converts a standard layout cut into an engaging experience.

4

Review & Collaboration

Step 04

I send you a private review link (Frame.io or Google Drive). You pin comments right on the timestamp, and I execute revision requests with precision.

5

Master Delivery

Step 05

Once you're 100% happy, I export in fully optimized high-resolution codecs (e.g., ProRes or clean H.264) tailored for YouTube, TV, or social platforms.
